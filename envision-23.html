All that you need to know about Envision'23:

IEEE and ACM proudly present a dynamic tech event merging innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship. This transformative conclave and tech-a-thon features inspirational keynotes, a startup conclave, networking opportunities, skill-building workshops, a startup showcase, and a resource hub. With the participation of IEEE SB, IEEE Comp Soc, ACM-W and ACM, this event brings together a diverse community of tech enthusiasts. Notably, investors will be present, offering opportunities for participants to pitch their developed ideas for potential investment, making this event an exciting convergence of innovation and business in the world of technology and entrepreneurship.

Networking Benifits:

Envision'23 offers unparalleled networking opportunities for participants to interact with industry professionals, gaining on-field knowledge and insights into the latest trends and technologies.
