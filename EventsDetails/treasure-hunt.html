TREASURE HUNT

Date: 11th Jan 2023

Time: 12:00 noon 

Registration Fee: ₹50 per team

Mode: Offline

Venue: AB1 Portico

Rules:
• Participants can be present in a team of 4 members.
• Everybody will be given to solve a riddle at the venue area.
• The first to solve it gets to move on in the game and go the next treasure to solve it.
• Likewise the event will have 5/6 treasures.
• The first one to solve all the puzzles correctly wins.
